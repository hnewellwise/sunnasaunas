Welcome to The Circle

We aren’t just a service, but a part of a growing movement to bring sauna culture to Portugal in a way that’s natural, accessible, and deeply felt.

In The Circle, you can:

  • Discover stories, rituals, and reflections from the sauna
  • Get updates on where we’ll be (markets, festivals, wild pop-ups…)
  • Follow the beginnings of the Portuguese Sauna Association
  • Learn more about contrast therapy, heat rituals, and cold exposure
  • Connect with others who believe in slowing down and sweating well

Join us in exploring the warmth and connection that sauna brings to our lives.
